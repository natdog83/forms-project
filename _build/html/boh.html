Bag of Hope¶

When your child receives a diagnosis of type 1 diabetes (T1D), it can be an overwhelming time. As you navigate this challenging period of adjustment to life with T1D, you can find helpful information and support through the JDRF Bag of Hope®. The JDRF Bag of Hope is filled with useful resources for both the child who has been diagnosed with T1D and his or her caregivers. Along with educational materials, we’ve included a special friend — Rufus, the Bear with Diabetes® — to show your child he or she is not alone while learning to take shots and test blood sugar. Resources in your JDRF Bag of Hope.

The BOH form was creatd in Gravity Forms and sends the data to the Salesforce Lead object.

Triggers¶

Trigger run when a lead is created with BOH as the lead type and does the following (details below):
  • Creates a contact for the parent contact record
  • Creates a contact for the child contact record and adds the contact to the parent household
  • Creates an Outreach request and links both the parent and child contact record to the outreach request
  • Automatically create new Additional Interest record upon Contact record creation
